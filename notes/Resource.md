### Attributes
___

1. Name
2. Email
3. Primary job title
4. Secondary job title
5. Status
6. Department
7. Location
8. Notes
9. Skills (Searchable tags)
10. Image
11. Availability (Number of hours per week)
12. Working days (Days of work. E.g. Some people work only Monday to Wednesday)

* A resource can be allocated to none or more projects
